Problem Solving:

  • Define problem clearly
  • Divide and conquer (Break up problem into smaller mini problems)
  • Mini problems are easy to solve
  • Keep the whole picture in mind
  • Keep track of what you have done and what you need to do

Being Lazy:

  • If it’s been made before don’t make it again
  • Don’t be greedy (being too lazy now may be a problem in the future)
  • Write no more than needs writing
